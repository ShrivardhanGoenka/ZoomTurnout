What is ZoomTurnout

Zoom Attendance is a plugin which can be used to take attendance during and after meetings. During meetings, it can display all the current participants in the meeting and all those who had joined and left the meeting at that instant. Attendance can be taken from a pre-uploaded list of names. These lists are uploaded by the user in excel format. After each meeting is over, it can be used to display a report of all the participants, i.e., the time when each participant first joined, the time when each participant left the meeting, total duration spent by the participant in the meeting and how many times did the participant leave the meeting. A flagged section is also available where it will display all the participants who were late to the meeting, who left early and also who did not spend an adequate amount of time in the meeting. The time which is considered to be late, early and the minimum duration can all be set by the user himself.

Why use ZoomTurnout?

ZoomTurnout generates reports after every meeting which highlights the entry time, leave time and duration spent in meeting of every participant. The teachers can set metrics such as the minimum duration every student must spend in class, how much time after the meeting has started is a student considered to be late, and how early is a student allowed to leave. If any participant fails to follow any of these criteria, they will be flagged by the app. Thus, the teachers do not need to waste their time tracking the entry and exit of every student and can focus solely on teaching.

How does it work?

ZoomTurnout uses a feature of Zoom Development called Webhooks. These webhooks send information to ZoomTurnout whenever an event such as a participant joins or leaves the meeting occurs and ZoomTurnout stores it and compiles a list of all such relevant events. These are then converted to readable format and into relevant sections such as reports, flagged participants, etc. All of this happens via the servers of zoom. Thus, it will not interfere with the working of the Zoom Client app on your device or on any of your participants’ devices. Thus, even if the server of ZoomAttendace crashes it will not harm your meetings. Also, if you enable this feature will in no way slow your zoom meetings or cause it to crash.
